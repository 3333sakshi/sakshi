BCA

The BCA course is a full time three years (six semesters) Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Application. The basic objective of BCA Course is to provide young men and women with the required knowledge and necessary skills to get rewarding careers into the changing world of Information Technology.The BCA syllabus focuses on database management systems, operating systems, software engineering, computer architecture, web technology and languages such as C, C++, HTML, Java, etc. Candidates aspiring for admission to the course can check the BCA syllabus below
